The WDDX Specification and WDDX Projects

The WDDX Specification is made up of documents and other materials that have been prepared and made available to the WDDX developer community. The WDDX Specification is currently sponsored by Macromedia, Inc. and is available from www.OpenWDDX.org. The WDDX Specification is expected to change from time to time; as used in this license, the term "WDDX Specification" refers to the most current released version.

WDDX Projects (sometimes just called "Projects" in this license) are programming language and technology-specific serializer/deserializer implementations based on the WDDX Specification. Projects may include source materials (which may be modified and distributed under this License) and non-source materials (which may not be modified but may be distributed under this license).
